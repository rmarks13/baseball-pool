How results & entries work

This pool is backed by a Google Sheet. Everyone's picks live in the Entries tab. Tournament outcomes live in the Results tab.

For players

Fill out the form on the Enter picks tab and hit Submit. Your entry shows up on the leaderboard within a minute or two. To change picks, ask the admin to delete your row, then resubmit.

For the admin

As games finish, open the Results tab of the Google Sheet and type the winning team into the matching column (e.g. r_UCLA for the LA regional, sr_sr1 for the first super, finLeft / finRight / champ for the finals). Every browser viewing this page will pick up the new results and recalculate scores on the next refresh.

Team names must match exactly (case-sensitive) — copy from the team lists if unsure.
